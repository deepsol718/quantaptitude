Relation between AB, BC, CA and ABC

If time required to do a piece of work by P1(person 1), P2(person 2), P3(person3) are A, B, C respectively. the speed
of P1, P2, P3 will be S1, S2, S3 respectively. The time required to do a work by P1 & P2, P2 & P3, P3 & P1 are x, y, z
respectively. T is time required by A, B, C all. We will find relation between them.

Note: The of individual person will be the reciprocal of their time. for example speed of P1 will be
S1 = 1/A.

1. If we add the speed of P1&P2, P2&P3, P3&P1 we will get the speed that would be twice the speed of all three if they
work together. Why?
because the total speed of all three is sum of speed of P1, P2, P3 and in the above statement we have added speed of
each individual two times.
2. If speed of P1&P2 is given and speed of P3 is given then the total speed would be the sum of speed by P1&P2 and P3
directly. Why?
Because In average speed we can not add two speed together but we have to first find the total distance and total time
then we find the speed but here in TIME AND WORK we add speed together because the amount of work is always same that is
